Keyboard & Game Controls

Quick reference for all games. Exact controls are also shown on each game page.

  • Backrooms: Pac-Man — W A S D move, Mouse look, Shift run, Esc pause.
  • Shadow Crawler — W A S D or Arrow Keys move, Shift sprint, E interact, Esc pause.
  • The Abyss — W A S D swim, Mouse look, Shift boost, E interact, Esc pause.
  • Nightmare Run — Space or Up jump, Left/Right move lanes, Esc pause.
  • Yeti Run — A / D dodge, W jump, S slide, Esc pause.
  • Blood Tetris — Left/Right move, Up rotate, Down soft drop, Space hard drop.
  • Séance — Mouse guide and click to select letters, Esc pause.
  • The Dollhouse — Click interact, drag items, number keys swap items, Esc pause.
  • Zombie Horde — Click place/select, W A S D pan, Q / E rotate, Esc pause.
  • The Elevator — W A S D move, Mouse look, E interact, Shift sprint, Esc pause.
  • Graveyard Shift — W A S D move, Mouse look, E interact, Shift run, Esc pause.
  • Web of Terror — W A S D move, Mouse look, E interact, Shift sprint, Esc pause.
  • Total Zombies Medieval — W A S D pan camera, Scroll zoom, click select/command, Esc pause.
  • Total Zombies: Rome — W A S D pan camera, Scroll zoom, click select/command, Esc pause.
  • Crypt Tanks — W A S D move, Mouse aim, Click fire, 1–8 upgrade stats, Esc pause.
  • Cursed Depths — A / D move, Space jump, LMB attack/mine, RMB place, E inventory.
  • Freddy's Nightmare — 1–8 switch camera, Space close monitor, E interact, Esc pause.
  • Haunted Asylum — W A S D or Arrow Keys move, Shift sprint, E interact, Esc pause.
  • Ritual Circle — Click cast, W A S D move, Q / E switch spell, Esc pause.
  • Cursed Sands — W A S D move, Mouse look/attack, Shift sprint, E interact, Esc pause.
